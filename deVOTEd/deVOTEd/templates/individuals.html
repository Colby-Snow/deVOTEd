Success Story of Volunteers!

As the only person in my family that can fluently speak both English and Spanish I've always had to translate documents for my parents and translate for my grandparents at the hospital. - Melanie

I had the opportunity to volunteer at a few different places, but my favorite was donating my time at Second Chance Animal Shelter. I really enjoyed it because the animals I interacted with and took care of were genuinely happy for my time and attention. I didn’t feel like I was “Working” at all. My Employer also allowed us to participate in that event so it was definitely a win win. -Heather

The robotics club hosted a stem night where we got to share interests. And I got to present to the kids. I did a demo on 3-d printing. This was exciting because it's cutting edge technology. We got to teach them how 3-d printing works and show them our creations using our 3-d printer. Sometimes it wasn't even the kids who were most interested, it was the adults and parents who were really excited. Everybody learned something there. -Colby

I found a service learning project at IHCC. It involved volunteering at the local library. I love working with kids so I decided to get involved and when I arrived they had me help with math homework with kids who were ages 12 to 15. They really apprciated my help. Tthe good thing is that I still keep in contact with them and their parents . I got to develop really important relationships and I fond memories of it. -Julduz

Find Yourself

Join DeVoted's mission and cause to increase local awareness about other's needs. The most genuine form of patriotism and loyalty we can show our nation is by taking care of home.

DeVOTEd is a dedicated program to encourage the participation of improving our communities. The most effective way to make a positive change in our lives is by improving the lives of others and the enviroment that we all live in. There is so much individuals as well as businesses can do to donate their time and energy for a cause that hits home and complements our identities as human beings.

Are you a business looking for a great way to improve your company's reputation and impact on the community? If so, you can start here! Bring your team in for meaningful experience. We work with many local companies and communities in order to host events.
